Ewatto Youths Launch Cooperative Society to Promote Farming and Small Business

Youths in Ewatto have launched a cooperative society aimed at supporting small businesses and farming ventures. The new group promises to pool resources, access loans, and create sustainable jobs for young people.

Speaking at the inauguration, the cooperative leaders urged members to embrace unity and hard work. They believe the initiative will reduce rural-urban migration.

Community elders praised the move, describing it as a positive step toward self-reliance and economic empowerment.
