Ewatto Secondary School Students Excel in WAEC Examination

Ewatto Secondary School has made headlines as over 20 of its students achieved distinctions in Mathematics and English in the recently concluded WAEC examination.

Parents and teachers praised the outstanding performance, attributing the success to hard work, dedication, and community support for education.

The success has sparked discussions on improving infrastructure in the school to ensure even more students can succeed in future examinations.
