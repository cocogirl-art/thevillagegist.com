Community Leaders Call for Urgent Repair of Ewatto–Ewohimi Road

Residents of Ewatto community have raised alarm over the deplorable condition of the Ewatto–Ewohimi road, which has been left unmaintained for years. Traders and motorists complain of frequent accidents and vehicle breakdowns.

Local leaders are appealing to Edo State government to prioritize the road rehabilitation project, as it remains the major link between Ewatto and neighboring communities.

Youths have threatened to mobilize self-help efforts to grade the road if government delays further, citing the road’s importance to economic survival.
